FAMI

Background: Fami is a well-known soy milk brand in Vietnam, cherished by families. The brand aims to target households with school-age children, positioning its soy milk as the perfect source of nutrition to fuel kids for an active day

Creative Solution: Many students feel sluggish in the morning, making it difficult to focus in class. Understanding this, Fami provides essential nutrients from soybeans to help kids stay energized and attentive. The radio ad plays with the musical notes “Do Re Mi Fa…” to create a fun, catchy connection with the brand name “Fa-Mi.” This familiar and playful wordplay will instantly resonate with listeners.

MILO

Background: MILO is a trusted choice for parents seeking nutritious drinks to fuel their children’s day. The brand aims to establish itself as the ultimate morning energy booster.

Creative Solution: While Fami adopts a warm, emotional tone, MILO embraces a dynamic and powerful energy. The radio ad introduces an exciting “Tournament” between two teams: “Students” and “The Morning.” This concept not only aligns with MILO’s brand personality but also adds an engaging, competitive twist that captivates the audience.

DECUMAR

Background: Decumar introduces its new product, Decumar Clean, targeting teenagers (ages 15-19) who need gentle yet effective acne care. The goal is to increase awareness of this new product among students.

Creative Solution: The ad creatively reimagines the classic Vietnamese children’s song “Rồng rắn lên mây” by incorporating famous female literary characters like Thị Nở, Tấm, and Mị. Regardless of their circumstances, these characters all share one thing in common: they must wash their faces before heading out. This playful and nostalgic approach, combined with an upbeat rhythm, ensures an engaging and memorable listening experience

MOBIFONE

Background: MobiFone launches a new membership benefits program and aims to spread this message to a wide audience

Creative Solution: The ad employs a riddle-based opening to pique listeners’ curiosity before seamlessly transitioning into the program’s details. This approach keeps the audience engaged while effectively delivering the key message.
