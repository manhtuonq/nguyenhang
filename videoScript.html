MoMo TVC

MoMo aims to raise awareness of the "Thổ Địa MoMo" feature during the Lunar New Year, encouraging users to explore it for finding the best dining spots and securing exclusive holiday deals.

This TVC cleverly adapts the traditional Vietnamese children’s rhyme "Rồng Rắn Lên Mây," immersing viewers in the nostalgic experience of searching for their favorite Tet eateries. By highlighting the ease of discovering restaurants that remain open during the holiday and accessing special promotions, the ad invites users to explore "Thổ Địa MoMo" on the MoMo app.
