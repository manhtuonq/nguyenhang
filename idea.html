CHỐT ĐƠN ĐẠI CHIẾN (2023) – MEGA GAME - Won 3rd prize

Background: Year-end is the peak season for shopping and dining, making it the perfect time to promote QR code payment behavior. This year, MoMo aims to establish itself as the go-to platform for QR code payments, emphasizing its ability to scan a variety of QR codes: ✅ VietQR (business payments) ✅ QR Payment (merchant transactions) ✅ My QR (personal QR codes) From Q3 to Q4, multiple campaigns will be launched to drive QR scanning habits, with the Mega Campaign acting as the biggest push in Q4. The campaign will target a diverse range of industries, including coffee shops, convenience stores, supermarkets, and street food vendors.

  • ○ Players become QR Code Masters, scanning to collect orders and unlock 3 “District”.
  • ○ The ultimate goal? Secure the Golden Ticket and win up to 10 billion VND in rewards

Core Gameplay

Endless Runner Mechanics – Inspired by Subway Surfers & Temple Run

Game Progression:

  • 🏙 Crispy Street (Food District) – Collect 1,000 orders to unlock the next level
  • 🛍 Bustling Boulevard (Shopping District) – 1,500 orders unlocks the final level.
  • 🎡 Trendy Town (Entertainment District) – Collect orders + assemble a Golden Ticket for the final reward round.

Real-World Actions Impact Gameplay: Scanning VietQR codes, referring friends, and making transactions earn extra game turns.

Engagement Strategies:

  • ○ Viral Elements – Players can steal orders, trade ticket fragments, and enter referral codes for bonus rewards

PICK YOUR PERK

Image Content

Background: The MoMo Referral Program introduces an exciting twist with "Pick Your Perk", giving users complete freedom to choose their rewards. Instead of receiving a fixed gift, participants first enter a personalized selection flow where they can handpick their favorite vouchers. Once their reward lineup is set, they move on to the next step—inviting friends to download the MoMo app. By putting choice at the center of the experience, "Pick Your Perk" transforms the referral process into a fun, rewarding journey. Users don’t just earn rewards—they curate them, making every invitation more meaningful and engaging. MY SOW: Develop key messaging and creative concept for the main KV to communicate the project effectively. Collaborate with Product and content production teams to craft mini-app content tailored to each user journey. Ensure a seamless and cohesive communication rollout across multiple touchpoints, including Webview, Website, Facebook posts, and Notifications, based on the established concept and messaging. Execute: Key Visual Concept: "Gift Grab Machine" For this MoMo Referral Program campaign, the main Key Visual (KV) is designed around the concept of a "Gift Grab Machine", symbolizing the power of choice. Instead of receiving a random reward, users are in full control—just like picking a prize from a vending machine or claw machine. The visual execution brings this idea to life: 🎁 A vibrant selection of gift boxes, each representing different reward options. 🕹️ A playful, interactive vibe, emphasizing the excitement of choosing. 📱 A seamless connection to the MoMo app, where users can invite friends and redeem their chosen gifts. By integrating this engaging gamified element, the KV transforms the referral journey into a fun, rewarding experience—where users don’t just get a gift, they get the gift they want.

Entry Points: Notification & Hub Access

The journey begins when users receive a notification from the MoMo app or navigate to the “Invite Friends” Hub within the app. Clicking on either entry point directs them to the Pick Your Perk program’s main screen

Choosing a Reward Path

Upon landing on the program’s homepage, users are presented with two options:
● “MoMo Picks for You” – Instantly receive a preset voucher bundle.
● “Choose Your Perks” – Customize rewards by selecting their preferred vouchers.

Selecting Voucher Perks

If users click ““Choose Your Perks”, they will be taken to a screen displaying various voucher categories. Users can browse and select the vouchers that best match their preferences.
